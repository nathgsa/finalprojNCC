A Filipino nationalist, writer and polymath active at the end of the Spanish colonial period of the Philippines.




He is considered the national hero (pambansang bayani) of the Philippines.


    Here is the timeline of Jose Rizal's life:

  • 1861

    - Rizal was born on June 19 in the town of Calamba in Laguna province. His father was Francisco Rizal Mercado y Alejandro and his mother was Teodora Alonso Realonda y Quintos.

  • 1861

    - Rizal begins school under the instruction of Justiniano Aquin Cruz at nine years of age.

  • 1871

    - Rizal Continues his education under the instruction of Lucas Padua.

  • 1872

    - Rizal entered St. Tomas University in Manila as a scholar.

  • 1877

    - On March 14, Rizal at the age of 16 obtains his bachelor of arts degree from the Ateneo Municipal de Manila with a general average of sobresaliente (excellent).

  • 1878

    - On January 6, Rizal enrolls in Philosophy and letters at the University of Santo Thomas (UST).
    - On May 21, Rizal passes the surveyor's examination (but is granted a license only after three years).
    - June 2, Rizal enrolls in preparatory and proper medicine at UST

  • 1882

    - Rizal first traveled to Europe.

  • 1883

    - He went to Parish for vacation.

  • 1887

    - Rizal returned to the Philippines and was involved in a land dispute. And the publication if Noli me tangere in Berlin.

  • 1891

    - Publication of El Filbusterismo in Belgium.

  • 1892

    - Exile in Dapitan.

  • 1896

    - Execution of Rizal in Bagumbayan.
